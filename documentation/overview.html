Project Overview

Abstract Ashart Gallery is a website which gives all the information about famous Artists and their artworks. the information include various artworks, Artist's Biography, Information about each art work.

Technology Used

  • ASP.NET
  • Javascript, JQUERY and AJAX
  • CSS and HTML

Content The project consists of Homepage where I have put a search option where users can search for Art Work or Artists Second page is Artist page where I have fetched Information about famous Artists and displayed their artworks with details . Third page is a short introduction about this page. Fourth page is dedicated to my own journey of artworks. I have also provided link to my net4.ccs.neu Homepage and Project Documentation

Further Motives I will be using Includes resources from the APIs provided by other museums. I will also include a page for Video Tutorials of painting and crafting over various medium . I will have section for getting information about various shows happening around the world
